Why Metrasensor

The cornerstone of our service is a self-learning neural network. It allows you to control all processes on your servers and in your applications. In case of a potential danger to your infrastructure, it is capable of warning you about possible problems and helps you to track system anomalies.

We use Prometheus exporter as a data source. It enables our sensors to integrate into your metrics infrastructure easily and safely. Installation of Prometheus server, Grafana, or other control, monitoring and visualization services is not required.

Right now we have already prepared about 20 sensors for the most popular services. These include PostgreSQL, Redis, MySQL, ActiveMQ, Cassandra, Jboss, Kafka, Tomcat, Weblogic, Zookeeper, Nginx, Apache, Ceph, CPU, DISK, MEM, NET, Docker, Elastic, Haproxy, Ping, Prometheus, RabitMQ. The number will only grow in time. We have put a lot of effort into making Metrasensor as user-friendly as possible. Connecting sensors to your services is made simple and comfortable.

We are using webhooks to notify your system about occurring events. It means a real-time receiving of information about all events in your system without making requests by yourself. The information will come regularly to the designated URL using POST requests. The number and queue of messages are also easily customizable.

In the near future

We are currently focused on the collection and analysis of logs. Logs are an immensely rich source of information that should not be neglected. It is too early to talk about the progress so far but perhaps in the future, the results of our work will surprise you. In any case, we hope so.
