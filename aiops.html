Artificial Intelligence for IT Infrastructure

AIOps (Artificial Intelligence for IT Operations) is a set of techniques and software used for IT infrastructure management and monitoring based on Artificial Intelligence.

Big Data and machine learning are the core technologies of AIOps. They provide IT specialists with an opportunity to shift away from the currently widely used approach of logging and tracing many separate events and to rely on machine learning for analysis of data received from monitoring systems.

IT functionality may include automation, IT services management, performance monitoring, and also event analysis and correlation. In other words, AIOps employs Data Science and machine learning in DevOps for more effective and productive results. Artificial Intelligence algorithms and machine learning reinforce the capabilities of IT specialists allowing them to focus on their essential tasks.

AIOps introduction factors:

  – The complexity of manual management of the modern IT infrastructure;
  – Data volume IT services require is growing exponentially;
  – Problems related to IT infrastructure must be resolved very fast;
  – Increasingly more resources are being moved outside the enterprise environment;
  – Despite application and service developers have increasing influence, IT is still responsible for the infrastructure's functionality.
