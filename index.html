Predictive Monitoring for your infrastructure and apps.

Metrasensor is an AIOps platform based on a predictive self-learning neural network. Capable, given enough data, of predicting events in your infrastructure up to several hours in advance. All you have to do is send the necessary data from your prometheus exporter to us and specify the thresholds. Then we will analyze the data coming to us in real time. And if the thresholds are exceeded or predicted that these values will be exceeded in the near future, we will immediately inform you about it.

*We are still in beta release. It's currently free.
